OpenGWAS

Empowering decision makers to maximise improvements to public health

The aim of OpenGWAS is to bring together disjointed sources of GWAS summary data alongside the suite of analytical tools that make value from those data. Building this coherence has drastically increased the scope of phenome-wide causal inference, alongside its reliability.
